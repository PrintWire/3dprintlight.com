About 3D Print Light

Covering additive manufacturing for electronics at every scale — from the desktop-printed enclosure to the conductive trace deposited inside the part.

OUR STORY

Electronics at the Frontier of Fabrication

3D Print Light covers the full spectrum of additive manufacturing for electronics — from the printed enclosure and connector to conductive inks, embedded passives, and fully printed multilayer boards.

Our editorial team includes hardware engineers, materials scientists, and RF designers with real project experience across printed-electronics prototyping, conductive-ink deposition, antenna design, and thermal management.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

To cover electronics AM from the first printed housing to fully embedded conductive circuits with equal depth — the mechanical, the material, and the electrical, in one place.

Our Vision

To be the authoritative, vendor-independent source for printed electronics — connecting engineers and product teams to the suppliers, inks, and machines reshaping how devices get built.

What Sets Us Apart
