Lighting Print Services

LED Diffusers & Optical Diffusion

Custom LED diffuser geometry for precise beam control and glare elimination. Internal light-guiding structures, micro-lens arrays, and prismatic facets. PolyJet multi-material for simultaneous clear-opaque zones.

02

Decorative Luminaire Shells

Parametric and organic luminaire forms in SLA photopolymer with custom surface texture. Wall, pendant, and architectural lighting forms with integrated mounting features — no injection mold tooling required.

03

Architectural Light Installations

Large-format light installation components via FDM and SLS. Translucent filament for internal LED glow. Complex space-filling geometric structures for gallery, hospitality, and commercial installations.

LIGHTING MATERIALS

Light Transmission Materials

Light behaves predictably through material — transmittance, haze, and scattering must be designed alongside the physical geometry.

  • ● Clear SLA resin — 90%+ transmittance, gloss surface
  • ● Translucent SLA — diffused glow, frosted glass effect
  • ● Tinted photopolymer — colored light transmission
  • ● Multi-material PolyJet — clear + opaque zones combined
  • ● Natural PLA/PETG — warm-glow FDM filament
  • ● TPU translucent — flexible diffuser applications
